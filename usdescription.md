## 4chan Archive Enhancer

A userscript that adds quality-of-life browsing enhancements to FoolFuuka-based 4chan archive sites.

**Supported sites:** archived.moe · thebarchive.com · archiveofsins.com

**Features:**
- Inline image expansion and hover zoom
- Fullscreen gallery with slideshow and swipe support
- Quote previews, backlinks, and threaded reply nesting
- MD5 image tracking, filtering, and bulk management
- Saved threads with cross-archive awareness
- Image-Only Mode and MD5 Filter Mode
- Custom board nav with smart cross-archive routing
- Batch image download and keyboard shortcuts
- Fully configurable settings panel
